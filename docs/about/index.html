About Me

Hello, I'm André, a multifaceted front-end developer with a degree in media design. My tech odyssey began as a kid in the 90s with a Commodore 64, my first love in computing – where I was more likely to be found trying to outsmart 8-bit challenges than doing my homework. This whimsical start led to over two decades immersed in graphic design and web development. I've had the privilege of working with a wide array of clients and have thrived in diverse settings, both as a collaborative team member and as an independent freelancer.

Currently, I am working at TalentAdore as a front-end developer, where I continue to apply and enhance my skills in this dynamic field.

My professional toolkit is extensive, with proficiencies in the Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, and modern web technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Node.js, MongoDB, React, and more. I am committed to creating seamless designs and user-centric development. Recently, I've been expanding my skills in 3D modeling with Blender and delving into web graphics through Three.js, showcasing my passion for learning and growth in the field.

My toolbox

As a technology enthusiast, I am always intrigued by the latest trends and innovations. My interests span across VR, gadgets, home automation, and virtually everything tech-related. This enthusiasm for technology is a driving force in my personal and professional life, pushing me to explore and experiment with new ideas and tools.

In my free time, I'm dedicated to activities that challenge me both mentally and physically. I enjoy learning chess, playing the piano, and engaging in sports like badminton, biking, and table tennis. Additionally, I'm a big fan of Rocket League, which combines my love for fast-paced gaming and teamwork. These activities not only provide a healthy balance but also contribute to my creative and analytical thinking.

My blog is a reflection of my diverse interests and projects in the tech world, covering a range of topics and explorations. It's where I share my insights, learnings, and experiences with a wider audience.

If you're looking for a developer with a unique blend of creativity, technical expertise, and a passion for the latest in technology, let's connect. I'm eager to bring my diverse skills and perspectives to your next project.

Best regards,

André
